My Journal

On March 13, 2023, I began my OJT (On-the-Job Training) with Rweb solutions, Corp. As a web designer, I couldn't be more excited about this opportunity. At the same time, I feel a bit pressured to prove myself in the professional setting. As a student, I've spent countless hours studying web design principles, coding languages, and graphic design techniques to be prepared in the IT Industry. Now, it's time to put all that knowledge into practice and gain real-world experience in the field.

On the first day they gave us an orientation regarding the rules and procedures. After the introduction, I was assigned to a mentor who would guide me throughout the internship. My mentor, Ma’am Danelle is an experienced web designer with a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of user experience. I'm grateful to have her as my guide during this journey. During those initial days, Ma’am introduced me to design software which is Adobe Photoshop, that’s the only tool they used in creating web designs and since Adobe Photoshop is not new for me, I focused on familiarizing myself with the design techniques and principles of user interface (UI) and user experience (UX).

As I adjusted to the role as a web designer. I already designed numerous landing pages for their clients and finished the task before the deadline given by my supervisor. As I gained more confidence and demonstrated my capabilities, Ma’am Danelle entrusted me with larger projects. I had the chance to design entire web pages, focusing on layout, typography, color schemes, and user interaction. The complexity of these tasks challenged me to think critically and creatively, finding innovative solutions to design problems while ensuring the websites were visually appealing and user-friendly. Throughout this period, I regularly sought feedback from my mentor and colleagues incorporating their suggestions to improve my designs. Their guidance and expertise were invaluable in shaping my design thinking and enhancing my ability to create meaningful and impactful web experiences.
